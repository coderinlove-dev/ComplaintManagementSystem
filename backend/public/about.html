About Complaint Management System

Bringing campus voices together — simply and digitally.

Our Mission

The Complaint Management System is designed for students and staff of our college to easily raise concerns, monitor issue progress, and receive timely resolutions — all online.

Our aim is to improve transparency, foster quick response, and ensure every member of our campus community feels heard.

Key Features

  • Simple, user-friendly interface to submit complaints in seconds.
  • Real-time status tracking: See if your complaint is pending, under review, or resolved.
  • Separate dashboards for students, staff, and administrators.
  • Data privacy & security: Your details and issues are handled with care.
  • Quick login/register with college email & credentials.

How It Works

1. Register or log in using your college email.
2. Submit your concern — instantly.
3. Track its progress from your dashboard.
4. Receive resolution updates and final response, all digitally.
